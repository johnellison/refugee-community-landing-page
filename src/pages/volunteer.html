Context

The power of design and technology has placed digital workers in a unique position for humanitarian crises—the refugee crisis in particular. If you have skills, passion and experience you can solve real problems for real people amidst the crisis and have fun alongside a community of passionate professionals.

Whether you're able to offer an hour a week or ten—we make it easy for skilled digital workers to contribute to refugee crisis projects.

Skills Needed

We have immediate need in the following disciplines:

  • Human-Centered / User Experience Design
  • Web Development & Architecture
  • Data Science & Mining
  • Ethnography & User Research
  • Agile Project Management
  • Social Media & Digital Marketing
  • Others*

*If your skillset isn't listed above and you'd like to get involved, please contact us. The need for passionate professionals with relevant skills is immense. We enjoy matching skilled workers with meaningful projects that make a significant impact on helping the refugee crisis.

How Does It Work?

We've worked hard to make sure that our volunteers can easily contribute to meaningful projects of the refugee crisis no matter where they are in the world. Here's the basic outline of the process:

1. request to volunteer

If you're a skilled digital worker interested in volunteering on Prosper Projects, please provide us with your details in the form below.

2. wait for us to review your request

One of our Prosper Community members will review your request and follow-up with you if we think it would be a good fit for us to work together.

3. receive an invite

If we think its a good fit for us to work together, we'll send you an invite to join our team. We use Trello to manage projects and Slack to communicate in real-time. Other than that, we have weekly stand-ups for project teams and monthly hangouts as a community.

4. contribute, learn, share (repeat)

We do our best to help volunteers contribute to a Prosper Project within their first week of joining the community. Hopefully you'll have learned something from contributing and can share what you've learned with the community. Given most of our tasks are broken into their smallest possible parts, you should be able to contribute easily whilst holding down your day job.

What To Expect

Here's what you can expect as a Prosper volunteer:

  • Access to the Prosper Community Slack Team
  • Access to the Prosper Community Trello Boards
  • Collaborate with a community of digital workers who share your passion for applying their skills to making the world a better place.
  • Invitation to weekly project stand-ups and monthly community hangouts.

Request To Join

If you are a skilled digital worker who would like to volunteer your time to help refugees prosper in Europe, please provide us with your details below.

Upon receiving your request, we will review the details you've provided. If it looks like we'd be a good fit to work together, we'll get back in touch with information on getting started as a volunteer.
